Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pilgrimage services

How far in advance should I book my pilgrimage?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for popular pilgrimage seasons and festivals. For Char Dham Yatra, booking 4-6 months ahead is advisable due to high demand and limited accommodation options.

What is included in the pilgrimage package?

Our standard packages include accommodation, temple entry arrangements, transportation between destinations, and the services of a pilgrimage guide. Meals, flight/train tickets, and personal expenses are typically additional unless specified in custom packages.

Are the accommodations near the temples?

Yes, we prioritize accommodations within walking distance or short commute from the temples. For remote locations like Kedarnath, we arrange the closest possible stays with convenient access to temple transportation.

Can I customize my pilgrimage itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized pilgrimage experiences. You can select specific temples, adjust the duration, choose accommodation preferences, and include special rituals or ceremonies as per your requirements.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We partner with verified hotels and transport providers, provide 24/7 customer support, offer emergency assistance, and ensure all our guides are trained in first aid and emergency procedures. We also maintain regular communication with local authorities in pilgrimage destinations.
